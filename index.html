Design Interview Framework

1

Clarify Requirements

Ask questions to understand scope, features, and constraints (QPS, latency, etc.)

2

High-Level Design

Sketch the overall architecture with major components and interactions

3

Deep Dive

Discuss specific components in detail (data models, algorithms, scaling)

4

Identify Bottlenecks

Analyze potential issues and propose optimizations

1 Common Questions

  • • Tell me about yourself
  • • Your greatest strength/weakness
  • • Describe a challenging project
  • • Handling conflict with teammates
  • • Why do you want this job?

2 STAR Method

Interview Preparation Timeline

1

Month 1-2: DSA Fundamentals

Master data structures (arrays, linked lists, trees, graphs) and basic algorithms (sorting, searching)

2

Month 3: Advanced Algorithms

Learn dynamic programming, backtracking, greedy algorithms, and graph algorithms

3

Month 4: System Design

Study distributed systems, databases, caching, and design patterns

4

Month 5: Mock Interviews

Practice with peers, use platforms like Pramp, and refine your communication
